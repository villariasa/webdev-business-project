About Me

I am a passionate frontend developer and design enthusiast currently pursuing my degree in Information Technology. I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics — it is about creating experiences that connect emotionally with the user.

This project, Villarias Motors, represents my vision of what a luxury automotive digital experience should feel like: immersive, elegant, and technically precise. Every detail — from the GSAP animations to the glassmorphism design language — was crafted to push the limits of what frontend development can achieve.

My goal is to one day work at the intersection of premium brand design and cutting-edge web technology, creating digital experiences that rival the best in the world.

About This Project

Villarias Motors was designed and developed as a comprehensive frontend project demonstrating mastery of HTML5, CSS3, and Vanilla JavaScript. The site features a complete design system built from scratch with custom CSS variables, GSAP-powered animations, a drag-to-scroll showroom slider, localStorage-based cart system, and a fully responsive multi-page architecture.
