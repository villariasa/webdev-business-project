🌸 About Me

Hi, I'm Deetreena — but most people know me as Rin or Aerin! I'm a Filipino student with a big passion for gaming, cute aesthetics, and building connections in online communities. I spend most of my free time hopping between Discord servers, playing Roblox or Mobile Legends, and occasionally diving into Valorant lobbies.

Outside of gaming, I love discovering new music, collecting cute digital stickers, customizing my Discord server, and finding the most aesthetically pleasing ways to organize... well, everything. I have a soft spot for pastel colors, cinnamon rolls, and anything that looks like it came out of a cozy cafe 🍩☕

"I built this cafe because I wanted a place where people could get good deals AND feel welcomed at the same time. It's not just a shop — it's a community~" — Rin
🎓 Academic Background

I'm currently a student taking up a technology-related course. I enjoy subjects that combine creativity and problem-solving — things like web development, programming, and digital design. This website itself started as a school project, but it quickly became something I genuinely care about and wanted to make the best I could!

I believe that learning doesn't stop in the classroom. Running Rin's Cafe Cinnamon has taught me so much about customer service, project management, marketing, and even basic coding and bot development — skills I'll definitely carry into my future career.

📅 The Cafe Story
August 2022
☕ Rin's Cafe Cinnamon was born!
Started as a tiny Discord shop offering discounted game top-ups to friends. The name was inspired by my love of cinnamon rolls and cozy cafe aesthetics~
June 2023
📘 Facebook Page launched!
Expanded to Facebook to reach more customers and share announcements, deals, and updates with a wider audience.
2023–2024
🤖 Started Discord Bot Commissions
Began offering custom Discord bots — eligibility checkers, calculators, and more — for server owners and communities.
August 2025
🧱 Roblox Community launched!
Opened an official Roblox Community to serve Roblox players directly — making group payout and Robux orders even faster and easier!
2025 → Present
🌸 Still growing, still cozy~
The cafe now has a full staff team including bots (Cinna & Mocha) and human staff (Ella & Crystall), with hundreds of happy customers and counting!

⭐ Fun Facts About Me
☕ The cafe is named after my nickname and a cafe from Sanrio, Cinnamon Roll is my favorite character.
🤖 I personally helped design the personality of our cafe bots (Cinna and Mocha) — they were meant to feel warm and friendly, just like the cafe itself!
🎮 I got into selling game credits because I was literally frustrated by overpriced top-ups. I decided to just learn how to offer them cheaper myself~
🌸 Everything about this cafe — the color scheme, the name, the vibe — was designed to feel like a cozy safe space for the community. That means a lot to me.
📖 This very website is part of a school project! It started as an assignment but I went all out because I genuinely love this cafe and wanted it to look amazing~
💬 I reply to DMs really fast — I'm basically always online. Come say hi on Discord anytime, I promise I don't bite 🌷
