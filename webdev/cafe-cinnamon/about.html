About Us ☕🌸


☕ Mission ☕
To deliver fast, reliable, and affordable game top-ups while creating a warm, cozy community where every customer feels welcomed, valued, and taken care of—just like in a real café. We aim to combine convenience, trust, and a touch of sweetness in every transaction, whether you're ordering through Discord, Facebook, or beyond.

🌸 Vision 🌸
To become a widely recognized for online gamers—known not just for great deals, but for genuine service, strong community bonds, and a unique, comforting experience that feels like home. We envision Rin’s Cafe Cinnamon growing from a small Discord shop into a trusted name across multiple platforms, serving players everywhere with consistency, care, and charm.

Our Journey 🗓️

From a tiny Discord shop to everywhere you play

☕
August 2022

🎉 Cafe Cinnamon Opens on Discord

It all started with a simple idea — bring discounted game top-ups to friends and the community. Rin's Cafe Cinnamon opened its doors on Discord with a small but passionate team ready to serve!

📘
June 2023

📘 Expanding to Facebook

The cafe grew bigger! We launched our official Facebook Page to reach more customers and make ordering even more accessible for everyone.

🧱
August 2025

🧱 Joining the Roblox Community

We opened our official Roblox Community to connect with the Roblox gaming crowd and offer Roblox Credits directly to players. The cafe keeps brewing new things!
