Frequently Asked Questions 🌸

Quick answers to common questions~

How long does delivery take after payment? ▼
Most top-ups are delivered within a few minutes to a few hours after payment confirmation. For Discord bot commissions, delivery time depends on complexity — we'll give you an estimate when you order!
What payment methods do you accept? ▼
We accept GCash, PayMaya, and other popular payment methods. Details are shared when you place an order on Discord or Facebook.
Is it safe to order here? ▼
Yes! We've been serving the community since August 2022 with hundreds of successful orders. We'll never ask for your account password — only the game ID or username needed for the top-up.
Can I request a custom Discord bot? ▼
Absolutely! Open a ticket on our Discord server or message us on Facebook with your requirements. We'll discuss features, timeline, and pricing with you~
What if there's an issue with my order? ▼
Don't worry! Contact us immediately via Discord (preferred) or Facebook and we'll sort it out as quickly as possible. Customer satisfaction is our top priority 🌸
